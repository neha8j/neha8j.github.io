About

Hey ! I am Neha, A semiconductor passionate who always wants to learn new things. I belive in hard work and never give up. I graduated from Shram Shadhna Bombay's trust college of Engineering and Technology in 2013 as an Electronics and Communication Engineer. I worked for chegg corporation for 2 years. I secured 1400 rank in GATE 2015. I post-Graduated from IIT Gandhinagar in 2017 as a microelectronics and VLSI Engineer. I joined GlobalFoundries in Feb'17 as an intern and then as a regular employee from July'17.
